Our Story

Natural Icecream is an Indian icecream brand owned by Mumbai-based Kamaths Ourtimes IceCreams Pvt Ltd.It was founded by Raghunandan Srinivas Kamath who opened its first store at Juhu in Mumbai in 1984. The chain recorded a retail turnover of ₹300 crore in the financial year 2020, up from ₹115 crores in 2015.The ice creams are manufactured by Kamaths Ourtimes Ice Creams and retailed by its subsidiary company Kamaths Natural Retail Pvt. Ltd.
